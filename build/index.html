Circles & Quarters

Goalies has two opinions

Circles

A balanced life is composed of managed 3 core aspects.

Personal life

Your personal life is your body, soul, and spirit.

Goalies is designed to have you remain aware of this aspect of your pursuits.

Relationships

relationships are the heart of life. Are you activitley doing things that help people?

Responsibilities

These are duties like chores, work, and asset management.

Quarters

Dividing the year into 4 quarters aligns with the four seasons and gives you 90 day windows to accomplish things.

the reason goalies is set up in 90 day windows is because anything worth doing takes time. And if you plan to do something worthwhile, then you need a substantial amount of time to do so.
